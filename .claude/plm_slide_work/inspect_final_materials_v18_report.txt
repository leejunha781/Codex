  2. Say the golden rule, then repeat it: "This is not a tool-replacement story. It is a governed, auditable, operations-aware decision loop that makes existing tools more valuable." This one line disarms most objections.

  5. Close by inviting the decision, not begging for it. State the ask once, clearly, then stop and let the room decide. Confidence is contagious.
